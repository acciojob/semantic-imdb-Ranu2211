The Shawshank Redemption
Actors Quotes
The Shawshank Redemption is a timeless story of hope, friendship, and resilience. The movie follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongfully imprisoned for the murder of his wife and her lover. Over the decades, Andy befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and eventually earns the respect of his fellow prisoners.

Actors

Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne - A banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank prison.

Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding - Andy's wise and loyal friend in prison.

William Sadler as Heywood - One of Andy's fellow inmates.
