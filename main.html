Summary

"The Dark Knight" is a gripping tale of heroism, chaos, and morality. Batman faces his greatest challenge yet as he battles the Joker, a criminal mastermind intent on plunging Gotham City into anarchy. With the help of Harvey Dent and Commissioner Gordon, Bruce Wayne must confront his own moral limits and make sacrifices to protect his city.
